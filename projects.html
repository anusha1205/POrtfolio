AirAware

- Second Year @VESIT Team project

AirAware is a comprehensive platform designed to assist individuals with respiratory conditions in navigating air quality concerns across India.

Key Features of AirAware:

  • Personalized Safety Alerts: Users can input their respiratory disease and desired location to receive customized advice based on real-time AQI (Air Quality Index) values, ensuring their safety.
  • Interactive AQI Map: A dynamic Leaflet map of India displays AQI values and the predominant pollutants for each location. Colors represent different pollutants, aiding in easy visualization and understanding.
  • Detailed Pollutant Information: Clicking on any location provides comprehensive data about pollutants, including charts and images, empowering users with detailed insights to make informed decisions.
  • Air Quality Trends: Users can track historical AQI trends, enabling them to plan their activities and outdoor exposure accordingly.
  • Real-Time Updates: AirAware delivers real-time updates on air quality conditions, ensuring users stay informed and proactive about their health.
  • Health Recommendations: Based on AQI levels and pollutant data, AirAware offers health recommendations and precautions for users with respiratory conditions to mitigate risks.
  • Community Engagement: AirAware fosters community engagement through forums and discussions, allowing users to share experiences, tips, and support related to air quality challenges.
  • Mobile App Integration: AirAware offers seamless integration with mobile apps, providing on-the-go access to AQI data and safety recommendations.
